# Source Review Policy Example

Public uploads should remain pending until an administrator reviews them.

Review should check source clarity, copyright risk, applicable year, private personal information, and whether the document should be marked as official, recognized group material, or community notes.

Only approved documents should be chunked, embedded, and made searchable.
