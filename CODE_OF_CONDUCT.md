# Code Of Conduct

OpenCampusKB should be a practical and respectful space for people building source-grounded knowledge tools.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Respect privacy and institutional boundaries.
- Avoid sharing private documents or personal information.
- Assume contributors may be students, alumni, staff, or independent maintainers.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Posting secrets, credentials, or private personal data.
- Encouraging misuse of institutional systems.
- Submitting copyrighted or private materials without permission.

Maintainers may remove content or contributions that violate these expectations.
