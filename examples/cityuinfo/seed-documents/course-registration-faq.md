# Course Registration FAQ Example

This mock FAQ demonstrates how reviewed content can be chunked into a source-grounded knowledge base.

## What should students check first?

Students should check official programme requirements, course offering lists, prerequisite rules, and registration periods.

## What if a course is full?

Students should follow the official waitlist or add/drop procedure. The assistant should not promise seat availability.

## What should the AI avoid?

The AI should avoid inventing registration dates, quota rules, or department-specific exceptions when the knowledge base does not contain reliable information.
