# Pre-arrival Checklist Example

This is mock seed content for the CityUInfo example. Replace it with reviewed official links or approved community notes before deploying your own site.

## Before Departure

- Check the official admission portal for enrolment deadlines.
- Prepare identity documents and admission-related letters.
- Confirm accommodation arrangements and arrival date.
- Save emergency contacts and campus office contacts.

## After Arrival

- Activate your campus account.
- Check email and student portal access.
- Prepare for course registration.
- Set up local transportation and payment tools.

## Answering Guidance

When answering from this document, remind users that dates and requirements may change and should be verified with official sources.
