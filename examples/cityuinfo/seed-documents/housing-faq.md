# Housing FAQ Example

This is example content only.

## What should students prepare for check-in?

Students may need identity documents, admission information, payment records, and any documents requested by the housing office.

## What should answers include?

Answers should include source names, applicable year, and a reminder to check the latest official housing notice.

## What should not be included?

Do not include private room assignments, personal contact details, or unverified group chat rumours.
